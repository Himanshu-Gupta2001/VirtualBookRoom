<DEV> Contact Deletion Phase 2: Create weekly contact deletion automation with new criteria (Spike GEE-54883)

As the data management lead, I want a new weekly contact deletion automation that covers all criteria for deletion and addresses new opt-ins of deleted contacts, so that we can ensure these processes are aligned with changes made as a part of the one-time contact deletion in [GEE-53015] Identify and purge duplicate subscribers - Jira and [GEE-58044] <DEV> Contact Deletion Phase 2: Purge "Not Active" Subscribers & implement auto suppression list (Spike GEE-54883) - Jira.

// Recommendations in Spike GEE-54883

SCENARIO 1: Weekly contact deletion automation
GIVEN a one-time contact deletion process has taken place ([GEE-53015]) // Contacts per SFMC; includes Contacts or Leads
WHEN the new weekly contact deletion automation runs
THEN it will perform contact deletion for the following groups:
-Contact does not have a channel
-Contact is no longer synchronized from Sales Cloud (Users, Leads, Contacts)
-Contact is a duplicate record in All Subscribers with email address as the subscriber key and is active
-Contact is a duplicate record in All Subscribers with email address as the subscriber key and is "Unsubscribed" or "Held"
- Contact has an All Subscribers record only for email address (no SF ID record) and is active
-Contact has an All Subscribers record only for email address (no SF ID record) and is "Unsubscribed" or "Held"
-Not sent any emails in the last 180 days
AND anyone in the exclusion list will be excluded from the final data extension for deletion

SCENARIO 2: Data backup
GIVEN the list of contacts to delete is available
WHEN the business has reviewed and approved the contacts to delete
THEN these records are backed up to SFTP\Marketing Cloud Exports
AND records are appended to the ContactDeleteArchive_PreviouslyDeleted data extension
AND all business provided data extensions are backed up // Automation: Duplicate Delete Data Archive

SCENARIO 3: Add email address of deleted contacts to an auto-suppression list
GIVEN an auto-suppression list was created for GEE-58044
WHEN the "not active" contacts to be delete are identified // Unsubscribed or Held
THEN the list of "not active" subscribers identified for deletion will be added to the auto-suppression list
AND they will not receive Marketing Cloud emails while they remain in the auto-suppression list

SCENARIO 4: Ensure consent status and hard bounces are processed for the deleted contacts
GIVEN the Consent Updates and Hard Bounce Suppression List automations can take up to 1 hour to complete
WHEN the contacts are processed for deleted
THEN all "Unsubscribed" contacts that require Content Status update to "Opt Out" are processed
AND all "Held" contacts that require CPE Usage Type update to "Invalid: Bounceback" are processed

SCENARIO 5: Delete Contacts
GIVEN the data extension for deletion has been approved
WHEN the contact deletion process is initiated
THEN said data extension will used in the process
AND the records will be deleted from Marketing Cloud with 0-day suppression

SCENARIO 6: Archive previous monthly automation
GIVEN the new automation has been created
WHEN the old monthly contact deletion is stopped
THEN it will be moved to the archive folder
